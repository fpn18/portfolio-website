Where the Magic Comes Together - Disney

MarketingMock Campaign Proposal

Overview

This conceptual Lunar New Year campaign explores how Disney could celebrate the holiday by combining cultural traditions with its core strengths: storytelling, emotional connection, and inclusivity. Through a cinematic hero film, personalized zodiac experiences, and limited-edition merchandise, the campaign creates shareable moments, engages audiences across generations, and positions Disney as part of the celebration.

Note: This mock marketing campaign was developed for educational purposes, this project is inspired by Disney’s brand identity but is not affiliated with or endorsed by Disney.
