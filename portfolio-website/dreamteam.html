About DreamTeam

DreamTeam is a student-led initiative at Imperial College London focused on enhancing campus life through innovative digital solutions. Our flagship project, the My Imperial Campus App, aims to connect students, streamline access to resources, and foster a vibrant university community. As a UX/UI Designer, I collaborate with a multidisciplinary team to create intuitive interfaces and engaging experiences that make everyday campus activities easier and more enjoyable for everyone.
