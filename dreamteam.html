My Imperial Campus App

An app created to improve student experiences on and off campus.

  • Type: Part-time Job
  • Role: UX/UI Designer
  • Date: Jul 2025 – Present

Problem

Enhancing the graduation experience for students at Imperial College London by addressing gaps in the current system and creating a more personalized, engaging, and memorable experience.

Research

To better understand students’ needs and experiences around graduation, I conducted a multi-faceted research approach:

  • Interviews: Conducted interviews with 3 students from various years, including Master’s graduates and current 3rd-year students, to gather insights into their expectations, challenges, and experiences related to graduation.
  • Observational Research: Reviewed graduation vlogs and TikToks to observe real-life student experiences, identify common pain points, and understand the emotional and social context of graduation.
  • Benchmark Analysis: Researched graduation-focused apps from other universities to identify successful features, workflows, and areas for improvement.

Ideation

Created sketches, tested low-fidelity prototypes, and refined flows based on user feedback...

Solution

Developed a streamlined app that integrates key student services into one hub...
