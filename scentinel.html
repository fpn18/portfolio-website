Scentinel

DESIRE award Winning Project

Overview

Scentinel is a smart scent diffuser designed to help beekeepers protect hives from pests and environmental stressors. Using targeted bursts of natural essential oils, it deters wasps and calms bees in a gentle, non-invasive way. The project brings together physical design, digital interaction, and visual identity into one cohesive, user-centered solution.

Note: This project included visual branding inspired by WWF to align with a sustainability brief, but was not developed in collaboration with or endorsed by WWF.

Problem

Urban beekeepers struggle to protect their colonies from pests such as wasps, hornets, and mice. These threats increase hive stress, reduce honey production, and can even lead to colony collapse.

Existing solutions are often invasive, unreliable, or unsustainable, with limited options suitable for small-scale urban setups.
