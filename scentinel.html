Scentinel

a smart diffuser system that listens to the environment around beehives.

  • Type: University Project
  • Role: UX/UI Designer, Video Production Lead, Brand & Visual Identity Designer
  • Date: Jan 2025 – Jun 2025

Problem

Urban hives are increasingly threatened by pests like wasps, mice and hornets. Current solutions are invasive, harmful and difficult to monitor.

Research

To better understand students’ needs and experiences around graduation, I conducted a multi-faceted research approach:

  • Interviews: Conducted interviews with 5 beekeepers from various areas and of different skill levels, including professional beekeepers, bee experts.
  • Observational Research: Visited Bee Urban - a social enterprise that aims to work with communities to improve the environment.
  • Benchmark Analysis: Researched various products on the market to view gaps in the market for bee protection.

Ideation

Created sketches, tested low-fidelity prototypes, and refined flows based on user feedback...

Developed a streamlined app that integrates key student services into one hub...
