Love Yourself Hard: e.l.f. x Katseye

Mock Campaign Proposal

Overview

This Valentine’s Day, e.l.f. Beauty partners with global pop phenomenon Katseye to redefine what love truly means, starting with self-love. Rooted in authenticity, confidence, and mental well-being, the campaign shines a spotlight on the importance of embracing individuality and prioritizing mental health in a world driven by perfectionism and comparison.

Note: This project included visual branding inspired by WWF to align with a sustainability brief, but was not developed in collaboration with or endorsed by WWF.
