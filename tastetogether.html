Taste Together

A global food box to educate healthy eating to families.

Overview

Taste Together is a monthly food subscription box that encourages families to explore global cuisines while learning about nutrition and culture together. It transforms cooking into a fun co-learning experience, promoting healthy eating habits and cross-cultural understanding through storytelling, recipes, and shared discovery at home

  • Type: University Project
  • Role: UX/UI Designer, Brand and Visual Identity Designer
  • Date: Jan 2024 – Jun 2024

Problem

Good nutrition is essential for children’s development — but families often lack accessible food education. Children rarely experience foods beyond their own culture, limiting both their dietary variety and cultural understanding. The challenge: Reimagine food experiences to promote health, well-being, and inclusivity.

Research

To understand the issue, I conducted interviews, school visits, and diary studies:

  • 👩‍🏫 Teachers shared how peers and parents shape children’s diets.
  • 👩‍👧 Parents and children discussed eating habits and lifestyle challenges.
  • 🏫 School visits revealed how dining environments influence behaviour.
  • 📔 A 7-day diary explored social media’s impact on food choices.

Key Insights

  • 👩‍👧 Parents are role models: Children mirror their parents’ eating habits; unhealthy choices at home affect children’s diet and preferences.
  • 📚 Limited parental resources: Few accessible tools exist for meal planning, understanding food labels, or affordable healthy options.
  • 🏫 School vs. home gap: Schools enforce healthy eating, but at-home habits often undo progress; stronger parent-school collaboration is needed.
  • 📱 Social influence: Social media trends and peer pressure drive children toward unhealthy snacks.
  • 🍫 Convenience & rewards: Sugary or quick-prep foods are used as rewards and consumed more during boredom or inactivity.

Ideation

From these insights, we asked:

💭 How might we make nutrition education a co-learning experience for families with young children?

I generated 15 initial ideas in response to this question — ranging from physical experiences to digital tools. Recognising that not all families have equal access to technology, I explored both directions but chose to prioritise physical concepts to ensure inclusivity and accessibility for all families.

Prototyping

Solution

Taste Together is a monthly food subscription service designed for families to explore, cook, and learn together.

Each box includes:

  • 🥗 All ingredients needed to prepare 3 meals for a family of 4, inspired by the cuisine of the featured country.
  • 🎲 A family board game that encourages interactive learning and play.
  • 🌍 A collectible passport where children can add a new stamp each month, tracking their culinary journey.
  • 📖 A storybook set in the featured country, bringing culture and traditions to life.
  • 📱 QR code linking to an interactive app for additional educational content and engagement.
